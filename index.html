We do high quality research about humans

Our goal at the Elegant Skeleton Research Institute is to investigate the aptitude of humans when they are presented with challenges. We are focussed on comparing the competency of humans while in teams with their competency operating alone.

Humans are fascinating

By investigating humans we are able to learn more about this fascinating, but baffling, species. We seek to gain a greater understanding of what makes humans most willing and capable to complete tasks.

Our human studies

We strive to undertake regular studies so that we can learn as much about humans as possible. We expect the Elegant Skeleton Research Institute to be the leading expert in humans by as early as 2563.
